Petr Bakla's Page

Listen Now

Petr Bakla was born in 1980 in Prague and finished the Faculty of Music.

" In his compositions, he uses basic, schematized musical units, such as standard scales or chords. He is interested in situations in which this "not-yet-music" becomes "already-music", he seeks positions and contexts in which seemingly banal and empty formations can acquire special expressiveness and energy.“
